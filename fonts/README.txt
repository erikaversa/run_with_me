Place your Noto font files (e.g., NotoSans-Regular.ttf, NotoColorEmoji.ttf) in this directory.

You can download Noto fonts from:
- https://fonts.google.com/noto
- https://github.com/googlefonts/noto-emoji

Recommended for emoji support: NotoColorEmoji.ttf
Recommended for general Unicode: NotoSans-Regular.ttf
